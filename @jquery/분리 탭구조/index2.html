Samsung's unique Twin Convection™ technology saves time in the kitchen. Upper and lower zones can be used simultaneously to prepare two different dishes, cutting overall cook time by 50 percent.

With Twin Convection™ technology you can heat only the upper zone for smaller dishes. Eliminating the need to heat the whole oven saves 25 percent more energy than conventional ovens.

With its divider removed, the NEO Twin Oven boasts a spacious 70L cavity. When used as one zone, the oven easily fits large dishes like a turkey.

Removable Oven Divider

Use the divider to transform the NEO Twin Oven into upper and lower zones for small meals, or to prepare multiple dishes simultaneously with separate temperature and time controls.

Maximum Energy Efficiency

The NEO Twin Oven, with its upper and lower convection cooking zones, offers advanced A-30% energy-saving technology for maximum home energy efficiency.
